## Creatinine


Creatinine is a normal waste product in your body, excreted when musclces are used and some of the muscle tissue breaks down. Normally, the kidneys filter creatinine from blood and remove it from the body in through urine. If there is a problem with the kidneys, creatinine can build up in blood and less may be released in urine. If blood and/or urine creatinine levels are not within range, it may be a sign of kidney disease.
